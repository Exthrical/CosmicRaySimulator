Particle Shower Observatory

Trace the cascade of high-energy primaries as they slalom through a stylized atmosphere. Protons and ionic nuclei convert their energy into pions and muons, while gamma rays pair-produce electrons and positrons. Each interaction obeys probabilistic rules derived from those seen in experimental air-shower detectors, so every burst feels unique.
